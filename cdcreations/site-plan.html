Content

Home page

What our website is: Have you ever had a craving for warm, moist cinnamon rolls or other baked goods that you wish could be delivered to you, where and when you want them? This website will provide a way for that wish to come true. There are many clear pictures of these mouthwatering options to choose from. There is also a rotating menu, that is updated on occasion, especially during the holiday season. Available options: Each option comes in different flavors, order sizes, packaging, and delivery options. You can choose if you would like to pick up locally, or have your order delivered to your address of choice. The choice can be made to have you order packaged for a gift, or basic packaging. If you are having it delivered through the mail, there is special care packaging to help keep your order from being destroyed in transit. If you are not ready to order or would like to gift some of these amazing treats, there is an option for a gift certificate.

Images for the Home page

Product Page

Product and flavor options: Product options include, but not limited to, pies, cinnamon rolls, pumpkin rolls, and sugar cookies. Pies come in the many flavors of apple, peach, rhubarb, cherry, strawberry, pumpkin, and blueberry. Our delicious and world-famous sweet rolls come in cinnamon, strawberry, orange, blueberry, and peach. Not to forget our moist sugar cookies and seasonal pumpkin rolls. Our menu rotates from time to time, so be sure to keep checking back for more to add to your favorites list. For allergy information, please email us directly. We can provide a list of ingredients to verify these treats are safe for you and your loved ones with special dietary requirements. Order sizes: Orders can be placed in different sizes. Sweet rolls come in a 9X13 pan and can be ordered as 6 extra-large, 12 large, or 12 small. Mini pies come in 6 packs per flavor and are shrink wrapped individually. Large pies are sold separate per pie and are shrink wrapped as well. Pumpkin rolls are sold as singles that are double wrapped to hold in moisture and flavor. Sugar cookies come in packs per dozen. They are packaged in an airtight bag and boxed to avoid breaking. As items are added, additional packaging and sizing options may be available.

Images for the Product Page

Ordering Page

Ordering: Once you have decided on what you want to order from the products page, you can pop on over to the ordering page and finalize your order. The prices are labeled for each product and shipping, or pickup prices are reflected. Have your address ready for the delivery option. You can complete the order form accordingly. Once you submit your order with an email address, you will receive an invoice reflecting your total and payment options. Tax will be applied according to your state tax. After payment has been received, we will process your order for the requested date. You can expect an email with updated information about your order as your order date approaches. Why you will come back: The ease of ordering these scrumptious treats, and the addictive flavors, will have you returning for more again and again. You will want to share this with your friends and family, knowing they are getting an amazing product. Here at Confection Delight Creations, we pride ourselves on providing an exceptional product that will not disappoint. We are a family own business that produce hand made products, that you cannot find from a big-box company or warehouse. We are one of a kind and strive to bring you the best homemade flavors money can buy.
