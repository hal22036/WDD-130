Product Page

Product and flavor options: Product options include, but not limited to, pies, cinnamon rolls, pumpkin rolls, and sugar cookies. Pies come in the many flavors of apple, peach, rhubarb, cherry, strawberry, pumpkin, and blueberry. Our delicious and world-famous sweet rolls come in cinnamon, strawberry, orange, blueberry, and peach. Not to forget our moist sugar cookies and seasonal pumpkin rolls. Our menu rotates from time to time, so be sure to keep checking back for more to add to your favorites list. For allergy information, please email us directly. We can provide a list of ingredients to verify these treats are safe for you and your loved ones with special dietary requirements.

Order sizes: Orders can be placed in different sizes. Sweet rolls come in a 9X13 pan and can be ordered as 6 extra-large, 12 large, or 12 small. Mini pies come in 6 packs per flavor and are shrink wrapped individually. Large pies are sold separate per pie and are shrink wrapped as well. Pumpkin rolls are sold as singles that are double wrapped to hold in moisture and flavor. Sugar cookies come in packs per dozen. They are packaged in an airtight bag and boxed to avoid breaking. As items are added, additional packaging and sizing options may be available.
