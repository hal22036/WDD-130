About Me

Jeni Hales

Mom, Mommy, Jen, Jenika, Babe

Hello everyone! My name is Jeni. I was born and raised in Provo, UT, but have currently been living in Las Vegas, NV for over 12 years. I have an amazingly supportive husband and 4 living children ages 2-10. They keep me super busy.

I served a mission in Louisville, KY in 2004. That allowed me to learn a new language and really get to know other cultures. I won't say it was the best thing I did in my life. I'll leave that description to being a mother. I am grateful for the mission experience though and wouldn't trade it.

I work part-time from home doing medical data entry. One of my goals to going back to school is to have a way to support my family while working from home. I am excited to learn more about web design and hopefully gain a career that I love.
